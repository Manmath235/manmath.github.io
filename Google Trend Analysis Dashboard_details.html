Project Description

  • Market Insights and Opportunity Identification: Leverages Google Trends search data analysis to identify consumer interests, emerging trends, and uncover new market opportunities.

  • Competitive Benchmarking: Enables benchmarking performance against competitors by comparing search popularity of relevant keywords and job roles.

  • Audience-Centric Content Strategy: Optimizes content strategy by understanding trending topics and creating engaging, relevant content tailored to the audience's interests.

  • Regional Market Understanding: Provides regional insights into consumer search behaviors across 60 countries, facilitating tailored marketing strategies for local markets.

  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Empowers data-driven decision making, forecasting, and strategic planning through real-time trend visualizations and in-depth analysis.

    What I Learned:

    1. Mastery of using parameters to filter and refine search data, enabling more precise and relevant analysis.
    2. Implementation of dynamic titles that update based on user interactions or data changes, enhancing the user experience.
    3. Integration of real-time data from the Google Trends API, ensuring the dashboard provides up-to-date insights.
    4. Development of clear, interactive visualizations, making complex data easy to understand and act upon.
    5. Insights into analyzing and comparing search trends across different regions, providing a global perspective on keyword popularity.
