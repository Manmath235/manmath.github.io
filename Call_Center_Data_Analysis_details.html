Project Description

  • Creation Process: Integrated data from call center operations and customer interactions using Excel and Power BI, enabling comprehensive visualization and analysis.

  • Analytical Insights: Uncovered trends, identified improvement areas, and developed predictive models to enhance operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

  • Stakeholder Engagement: Collaborated closely with stakeholders to tailor the dashboard to their needs, fostering a culture of collaboration and ownership.

  • Usability and Accessibility: Designed an intuitive interface with customizable visualizations and interactive features to facilitate data-driven decision-making for both technical and non-technical users.

  • Continuous Improvement: Implemented regular updates based on user feedback and evolving business needs to ensure the dashboard remains relevant and effective.

    What I Learned:

    1. Proficiency in data integration, analysis, and visualization using Excel and Power BI.
    2. Insight into call center operations and customer behavior analytics.
    3. Importance of stakeholder engagement for project success.
    4. Principles of user-centric design and usability.
    5. Value of continuous improvement for staying ahead in a dynamic business environment.
