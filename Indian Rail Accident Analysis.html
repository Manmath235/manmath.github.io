Project Description

  • High-Risk Area Identification: Identify high-risk areas and accident-prone routes based on the geographical analysis, enabling targeted safety measures and resource allocation.

  • Accident Cause Prioritization: Prioritize safety initiatives and investments by highlighting the most common causes of accidents, such as derailments, collisions, and sabotage incidents.

  • Emergency Preparedness Enhancement: Enhance emergency preparedness and response by understanding the severity and impact of accidents, including casualties and infrastructure damage.

  • Maintenance Protocol Optimization: Improve maintenance and inspection protocols by analyzing accident patterns over time, leading to better preventive measures.

  • Safety Regulation and Technology Guidance: Guide policymakers and railway authorities in developing effective safety regulations, public awareness campaigns, and technological upgrades based on the report's actionable recommendations.
